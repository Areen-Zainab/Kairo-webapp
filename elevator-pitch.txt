VERSION 1 — THE RELATABLE HOOK
(Best for: booth pitches, demo days, investor meetings)

Every meeting ends the same way.
The call drops, and within minutes someone asks:
"Wait, what was I supposed to do?", "What was decided?"

That's not a people problem. That's a memory problem.

Every meeting produces decisions, action items, and context.
And the moment the call ends, all three disappear.

Kairo fixes that.

It joins your calls silently — Google Meet, Zoom, whatever.
It transcribes in real time, and while the meeting is still happening,
it's already extracting every decision, task, and deadline.

By the time you leave, your tasks are assigned,
your board is updated, and everything is saved —
linked to every meeting that came before it.

No manual notes. No follow-up threads.
No "I thought you were handling that."

That's Kairo.
